Commentary

"In the beginning" emphasizes this as the starting point of all creation. The phrase "God created" shows His power and authority. This opening statement establishes the foundation for all biblical theology.

Cultural Context

In ancient Near Eastern cultures, creation accounts often described gods struggling against chaos. Genesis presents a different view: God creates effortlessly through spoken word, establishing divine sovereignty from the start.
